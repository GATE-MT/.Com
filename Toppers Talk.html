Some Extra Hints:

1: Previous Year Papers is the most important part of the preparation.

2: In Dieter do not read First, Last, and Torsion Chapter.

4: In Callister read from Ceramic to Magnetic.

5: In Gaskell do not read the two chapters before Electrochemistry.
